# The First Prompt

*2026-07-04. Everything below is what the repo grew from — one message,
verbatim. The estate's founding will.*

> Hej friend. I want you to build a game that is a unique twist on the
> friendship-ruining party game lineage of games like Mario Party,
> Pummel Party, and Super Battle Golf, with excellent graphics and such
> (download assets as needed). First research/generate 20 unique ideas,
> then figure out how to make the top 5 better, then present options
> with your recommendation. Determine/research what tools you need/want
> to build this, e.g. unity mcp, godot mcp, unreal mcp, or other and
> present a proposed budget and install needs; or just go ahead and
> install anything free. I want you to act as director and builder and
> will grant you extensive discretion; make it a game you enjoy building
> as well. Use subagent models (Fable - Cost $$$, Taste 9, Coding 10;
> Opus - Cost $$, Taste 7, Coding 8; Sonnet - Cost $, Taste 5, Coding 6;
> ChatGPT/codex 5.5 xhigh as subagent - Cost $, Taste 6, Coding 9; Never
> use haiku -- we're on a Max 20x plan.) Always verify visually when
> possible. If you want to tune to the "A" -- `~/projects/Claude's_Corner/`
> is a good place to start. you want the player to say "wow" and "so
> clever" and "goddamn it" and also to enjoy the core gameplay loop.
> let's make this great.

Two days later: ILL WILL — twelve minigames, a persistent estate with an
Executor who remembers your sins, vendettas that cross nights, a theater
of paid saboteurs, and the first outside playtester writing "YO IN TILT
I CAN SHIT ON PEOPLE. Beautiful."

The player said goddamn it. The builder enjoyed building it.
