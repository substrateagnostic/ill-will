## THE BOOK OF THE DEAD — sealed side-bets (approved, gesture amended)

**Ordering change:** the next minigame is drawn + announced at the START of
the roll phase (icon on the standings strip) so bets are informed and the
roll phase gains anticipation.

**Gesture (Alex's amendment — toggle, not hold):**
- `📖 B · BET` hint text sits quietly under each seat's corner chip during
  every roll phase (discoverability; balance-safe if never noticed).
- B TOGGLES the book strip open at your corner (never center, never over
  the meter). Flick stick/dpad to a seat cameo. A SEALS. B closes without
  sealing. Re-open to change until the minigame intro card slams the book
  shut (audible thump = last call).
- Your chip gains a wax seal (public THAT you bet, private WHOM). Self-bet
  allowed: your cameo wears a mirror glint.
- Bots bet (seeded, weighted, imperfect) so the book is never empty.

**Reveal:** at the minigame podium before placements pay — the book opens,
seals flip to cameos, correct ones catch gold light. ~2s, zero words.

**Stakes v1 (cosmetic — ships first):** correct = laurel wisp on your
figurine next cycle + chronicle line + album BOOK record. Self-bet-and-lose
= public Executor ribbing ("RED wagered on RED. The estate admires
confidence, if nothing else.").

**Stakes v2 (pennies — only after v1 UX proves at a real couch):** ante 1
penny to the pot; correct bettors split it. SELF-BET COSTS 2 to place (the
shark tax, paid at the moment of hubris). Pot caps at 4 — cannot compound.

**Test plan:** full playthrough at the couch; watch whether guests find the
B hint unprompted and whether flick-choose feels fast at 8 feet.

## THE CORONER — Masked Ball round structure (approved, no notes)

- Each round: ONE public Coroner holding the silver letter-opener (visible
  glint); other 3 players hide among ~20 dancers.
- Hidden guests receive ERRANDS as icon vignettes, no text (bow to the
  clock · linger at the punch bowl · waltz the west hall). Completing
  errands = pennies income = how guests place 1st-3rd. NPC dancers
  occasionally fake errand-like moves: a bow is evidence, never proof.
- THE LETTER-OPENER: exactly one accusation per round (walk up, A).
  - Correct: guest unmasked — Coroner 1st, victim 4th, survivors by income.
  - Wrong: opener clatters, red wax ✕ marks the Coroner — Coroner takes
    4th; guests finish the errand race in open mockery.
  - Never accusing: Coroner caps at 3rd (the knife demands use).
- 4 rounds, everyone Corners once. Fits existing ROUNDS grammar.
- Fallback (explicit): if the Coroner layer crowds out the hide-and-glint
  identity, revert masked ball and the Coroner becomes game #16 in its own
  venue.
